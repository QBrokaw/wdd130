Content

Home page

Main Site This site will be dedicated to the fictional character “Simon Templar” aka “The Saint.” Simon Templar is a good man that helps catch thieves, murders, criminals, and anyone doing bad things. The main page will be an overview of who is, what his main story line is about and a history of the books, tv shows, movies and music. It will be about Leslie Charteris the author of the original books. These books were published between 1928 and 1963. I would also like to embed YouTube clips. One big part of The Saint is the stick figure that was on the cover of the books and carried into the tv shows and the movies, I would like to include multiple images on the main page of The Saint stick figure. One other idea I have is to either have the tv theme song play when you first come to the site or embed a YouTube clip so that you can listen to it. There has also been a few revisions of the theme song and I would like to find a place to show them off too. Orbital has a great version.
